About Me

Let's get to know each other

I'll start...

I enjoy problem solving, innovating, and empowering others. My lifelong love of learning has led me on a journey to explore the intersections of technology and philosophy, and to uncover ways to protect our autonomy in the digital age. My background in business intelligence, risk analysis, and corporate strategy provides me with a unique perspective on these challenges.

I feel a sense of urgency to guard against the dangers of echo chambers and ensure that our thoughts remain our own. With experience working in the strategy arm of the development firm behind the Cardano blockchain (IOG), I am well-versed in the power of decentralization and distributed networks to resist oppression, promote financial inclusivity, and ensure transparency. I believe that these tools can help us safeguard our future and protect the autonomy of our minds.

So, why am I here? Well, I'm on a mission to make a positive impact in the world through the power of technology. My hope is to share my insights, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to a brighter, more equitable future for all.

In my spare time I also enjoy reading, photography, mycology, playing chess, and exploring. I am an alumni of Phillips Exeter Academy and the University of Wyoming.
