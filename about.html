About Me

Let's get to know each other

I'll start...

I'm a curious mind with a knack for problem-solving, innovation, and empowering others. My lifelong passion for learning has led me to explore the fascinating intersections of technology and philosophy, specifically how they impact our lives in the digital age. With a background in strategy, marketing, and project management, I bring a unique perspective to these challenges.

I'm driven by a sense of urgency to protect our autonomy and help ensure that our thoughts remain our own in a world increasingly influenced by profit-driven corporations and echo chambers. Having worked in the strategy arm of the development firm behind the Cardano blockchain, Input Output, I have a deep appreciation for the power of decentralization and distributed networks. I believe these tools can promote financial inclusivity, help people resist oppression, and ensure transparency; ultimately protecting our collective future.

Driven by a passion for positive change, I'm on a mission to harness the power of technology to create a brighter, more equitable future for all. By sharing my insights and connecting with like-minded individuals, I aim to foster collaboration and innovation, ultimately contributing to a world where opportunity and access are truly inclusive.

When I'm not pondering the digital landscape, I enjoy reading, photography, mycology, playing chess, and exploring the great outdoors. I'm an alumnus of Phillips Exeter Academy and the University of Wyoming.
