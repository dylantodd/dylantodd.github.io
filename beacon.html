Beacon

Suicide Prevention

Beacon is focused on extending the reach of traditional suicde prevention efforts through the development of a mobile application that efficiently and effectively reaches individuals struggling with suicdality and suicidal ideation. The primary focus is to de-escalate individuals, and connect them with follow-up services.

The de-escalation system will guide the user through tiers of support, depending on the severity of suicidal ideation. Throught every tier, the goal will be a combination of de-escallation and monitoring. If EMS needs to be alerted, along the way, then that will be possible through the app at every stage.
