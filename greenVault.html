Green Vault

Branding and Development

The idea behind the Green Vault branding was to create an iconic logo focused on highlighting security within a cubic structure. Both the "G" and the "V" of Green Vault are designed to stand out, and the logo shape is designed to represent a safe.

I developed the logo, business branding and infographics above. The infographics have been stripped of all sensitive and proprietary information. They are used to relay architectural principals and design aspects of the business's current project.
